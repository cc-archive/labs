In December 2008, Creative Commons hosted its second technology summit at MIT. Additional information and links to slides may be found in the wiki.

Download

The CC Technology Summit is available in OGG and MPEG formats at the Internet Archive. Audio files in FLAC, OGG, WAV and MP3 are also available from the open source audio collection.
